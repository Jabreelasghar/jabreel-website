# SEO Plan

This document records long-term SEO direction for the site. It is not public site content.

## SEO Positioning

The site should build search visibility around accurate academic themes rather than promotional personal-brand claims.

Primary topic areas:

- Dr Jabreel Asghar
- AI governance in higher education
- Responsible AI in higher education
- Assessment integrity
- Generative AI and assessment
- AI-assisted academic writing
- Educational quality in higher education
- Academic communication

## Metadata Principles

- Metadata should be factual and restrained.
- Avoid exaggerated claims.
- Use page-specific titles and descriptions.
- Do not claim publications, talks, or resources exist before they are real.

## Page Structure

Each major area should eventually have a clear overview page:

- Responsible AI in Higher Education
- Assessment Integrity
- Educational Quality
- Publications
- Frameworks & Resources
- Teaching
- Insights
- Contact

## Content Growth Strategy

Search visibility should grow through accurate, substantive content:

- Publication records with correct citation details
- Research-informed insight articles
- Publicly available frameworks and guides
- Teaching and academic communication content
- Confirmed talks or presentations

## Avoid

- Keyword stuffing
- Promotional claims
- Generic AI content
- Thin pages with invented authority
- Dated content without actual publication dates
- Misrepresenting topic pages as published articles
