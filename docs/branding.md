# Branding

This document records the website's academic identity and positioning. It is not public site content.

## Core Identity

Name: Dr Jabreel Asghar

Approved positioning:

- University Lecturer in English and Academic Communication
- Researcher in AI governance in higher education
- Assessment integrity and quality specialist
- Curriculum and assessment designer
- Fellow of Advance HE (FHEA)

## Brand Impression

The site should feel like the professional website of a credible academic researcher whose work bridges AI governance, assessment integrity, and higher education practice.

It should not feel like a commercial consultancy, personal brand funnel, marketing site, or speaking-services page.

## Voice

Use a serious academic tone:

- Clear
- Precise
- Restrained
- Evidence-informed
- Professional
- Accessible without becoming promotional

Avoid:

- Visionary
- Thought leader
- Transforming education
- World-class
- Passionate
- Disruptive
- Game-changing
- Empowering institutions
- Commercial service language

## Approved Themes

- Responsible AI in higher education
- Assessment integrity
- Educational quality
- Academic writing and discourse
- Curriculum and assessment design
- Evidence-informed higher education practice
- Institutional governance, when framed carefully and factually

## Positioning Boundaries

Do not invent or imply:

- Consultancy services
- International keynote speaker status
- Advisory services
- Media appearances
- Grants or awards
- Institutional impact claims
- External leadership roles
- Frameworks or resources that are not yet public

## Calls to Action

Preferred CTA framing:

- Contact
- Academic collaboration
- Research enquiries
- Publications
- Teaching
- Frameworks & Resources

Avoid CTA framing that sounds commercial, such as:

- Book a consultation
- Hire me
- Invite a keynote
- Transform your institution
- Strategy session
