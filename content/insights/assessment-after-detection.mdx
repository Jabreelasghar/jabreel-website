## Insight

Detection can play a limited role, but it cannot carry the full burden of academic integrity. Universities need assessment designs that clarify acceptable AI use and make learning processes visible.

## Implication

The central question becomes not only whether AI was used, but what learning evidence the assessment can credibly support.
