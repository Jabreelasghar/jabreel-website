## Purpose

Use this checklist as a starting point for conversations about responsible AI adoption in universities.

## Areas to Review

- Institutional principles and acceptable use
- Ownership of risk, assurance, and review
- Assessment and academic integrity implications
- Staff development and student guidance
- Evidence gathering and evaluation

## How to Use

Adapt each section to local policies, regulatory expectations, and disciplinary requirements.
