## Purpose

This template helps programme teams review assessment tasks in light of generative AI access.

## Audit Prompts

- What learning evidence does the task produce?
- Where might AI use be appropriate, restricted, or required?
- How are students expected to disclose or document their process?
- What feedback practices support learning rather than compliance alone?
